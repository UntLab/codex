AI hints
Mode: Strict – closer to exact filters.
  • Be explicit: “from Germany, revenue > 20 000, last 6 months”
  • Use business language, not formulas.
  • You can reference columns by meaning, not exact names.
Tip: Try something like
“Find all clients in Baku with more than 5 shipments this quarter”
The system learns from your prompt style (short, detailed, geographic, financial) and can reuse it in suggestions.
